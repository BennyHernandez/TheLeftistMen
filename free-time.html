Introducing: Free Time

Free Time is a musical created by The Leftist Men that tells the story of four high school boys struggling to find a balance between their academic responsibilities and their desire for leisure time. The show features catchy tunes, powerful lyrics, and a thought-provoking message that resonates with audiences of all ages.
The plot follows the boys as they navigate the challenges of high school life, encountering various characters along the way, including a strict drama teacher who pushes them to their limits, a rebellious classmate who encourages them to let loose, and a wise janitor who teaches them the value of hard work and perseverance. Through a series of heartfelt solos and musical numbers, the boys express their frustrations, dreams, and hopes for the future, ultimately learning that itâ€™s essential to make time for the things that matter most.
The Leftist Men recently announced the addition of a new cast member, Emma, who will be taking on the role of the drama teacher. Emma has a long history with The Leftist Men, having worked with them on various productions and projects over the years. Her casting was an easy decision for the team as they were seeking someone who embodied the spirit of Free Time as much as they did.
The Leftist Men, along with Ike and Emma and are eager to present it to the world. They are eagerly anticipating the world premiere on May 31st, and audiences can expect a moving and inspiring production that speaks to the universal struggles of adolescence and the importance of balancing hard work with leisure time.
